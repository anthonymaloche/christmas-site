The Coffees:

Madcap "Winter Solstice"

Roasted in Grand Rapids, MI this coffee happens to have a pretty cool name. But can the flavor live up to the name?

Tasting notes: "Pleasant cranberry brightness, tempered by hints of baking spice and a dense, raisiny sweetness"

Atomic "Holiday Blend"

These beans come to us from the northeast town of Salem. From carrying a caped Jack around the cobbled streets in this strangely touristy town, fighting off racoons at our haunted campsite near an abandoned swingset, and our morning stroll to a mysteriously delapidated building emblazined with the words "Slay Yaya', Salem definitely scared us. But one thing that didn't scare us off was their coffee roaster, Atomic. While the "Holiday Blend" is not one we've tried yet, we can say that this roaster delivers on all the other coffees we've brewed up.

Tasting notes: "Berry sweetness and boozy mulled wine acidity perfectly balance a smooth, chocolaty body in this delicious seasonal blend."
